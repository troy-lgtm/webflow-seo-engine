# SEO Engine Goals

## North-star objective
Maximize qualified self-serve quotes and enterprise meetings from organic traffic while preserving brand/design consistency.

## Business goals
- Grow self-serve quote starts from SEO landing pages.
- Grow booked enterprise fit calls from high-intent commercial keywords.
- Increase pipeline quality (qualified submissions, not raw leads).

## SEO goals
- Win high-intent SMB and enterprise keyword clusters.
- Own comparison/alternative intent where buying decisions happen.
- Improve LLM answer-surface coverage using concise answer snippets and entity-rich language.

## Conversion goals
- Increase page CTA click-through rate (Book + Quote).
- Increase start rate (quote/book flow starts per session).
- Increase submit rate (completed forms per session).

## Quality and brand constraints
- Content-only optimization; no layout/CSS/component edits.
- No unverifiable or defamatory claims.
- Keep messaging useful to real operators (SMB and enterprise logistics leaders).

## Measurement framework
Primary KPIs:
- Organic sessions by target cluster
- CTA CTR = (book clicks + quote clicks) / sessions
- Flow start rate = (book starts + quote starts) / sessions
- Submit rate = form submits / sessions

Secondary KPIs:
- Rage click rate (Clarity)
- Dead click rate (Clarity)
- Quick back rate (Clarity)
- Scroll depth (Clarity)

## Learning loop SLA
- Weekly: ingest GTM + Clarity, produce optimization backlog, apply safe edits to top pages.
- Bi-weekly: publish approved optimized pages.
- Monthly: prune low-performing keywords and expand winning clusters.
